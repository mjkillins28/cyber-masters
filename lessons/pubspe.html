Public speaking is a great skill to have, whereby knowing how to publicly speak will makes you a leader worth following. Public speaking may seem hard at first, but the more you practice, the easier it will be to understand how to do it. First you have to know what you will talk about this will require you to do your homework on the event or situation. Then work on projecting and modulating your voice, you can do this by reciting your speech out loud. With that said then you work on posture you can accomplish this by using S.L.A.N.T (Sit up, Lean forward, Ask and answer questions, Nod your head, Track the speaker). Finally, remember that practice makes the master, so practice as much as possible.
